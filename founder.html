Khan Imran Rahie's Story

Khan Imran Rahie is the Founder and CEO of Shahnaz Med — a modest medical wear brand built on a profound understanding of what women in healthcare truly need. His journey began not in a boardroom, but in observation: witnessing first-hand how women struggled daily with scrubs that offered neither adequate modesty nor genuine comfort.

Driven by a clear purpose, Khan Imran launched Shahnaz Med with a mission to design medical wear that truly understands the needs of women. He identified what most brands ignored — short lengths, improper sleeve coverage, and uncomfortable fits that left women feeling unseen and underserved in their own workplaces.

Under his leadership, Shahnaz Med has been built from the ground up with one goal: to provide scrubs with better coverage, comfortable fits, and practical designs — so that every woman can feel confident, dignified, and at ease during her work. His vision is to make Shahnaz Med the most trusted modest medical wear brand in the region and beyond.

His Philosophy

Building with Purpose

Solving Real Problems

Shahnaz Med was not built on guesswork. Khan Imran identified a genuine, underserved need — women who couldn't find modest scrubs — and built an entire brand to address it with care, research, and precision.
